Connect Any Four

This is Connect Any Four game made by Tanzeel.Below are the instructions that must be followed

  • Game starts with the turn of firs player and alternates
  • Click on the coloumn so that stone of the colour red/yellow falls into that coloumn
  • The player who successfully connects the four cells horizontally or vertically or diagonally first will win the game
  • Whenever a player wins the game ends and you can restart it by clicking the restart button
